O, unifying theories of consciousness

We believe that “consciousness” and the difficult problems surrounding it cannot be solved in isolation. They require the assimilation of knowledge from a wide range of sources throughout human history. With the advent of AI, this goal has become much more achievable. O is an effort to unify all theories of consciousness by bringing together insights from scientific theories of consciousness, quantum physics, psychology, neuroscience, philosophy, religion, and spirituality. The process involves creating a PDF that presents a common theory of consciousness, using information not only from empirical research but also from social media, the internet, and books in all the areas mentioned above. By using a large language model (in this case ChatGPT) a "deep research" is conducted from time to time, and a PDF is produced. This PDF serves as the “Knowledge Source” for an interactive GPT, which can answer and explain questions about the unified theory. There will also be a GitHub page to maintain the PDF and any updates to “O”.
